About the Workshop

The Columbia Workshop in New Empirical Finance brings together junior researchers working at the intersection of structural econometrics and finance. The workshop focuses on innovative empirical approaches to studying financial intermediation, credit markets, household finance, and asset pricing with frictions.

Founded in 2018, the workshop has become an annual gathering for scholars using structural demand estimation, equilibrium models, and revealed preference methods to advance our understanding of financial markets.
