About the Workshop

The Columbia Workshop in New Empirical Finance brings together junior researchers who focus on innovative empirical approaches to studying financial markets. Founded in 2018.

We invite papers that combine theory and data to study financial markets. We favor work where economic reasoning guides identification and interpretation, and welcome a broad range of topics related to financial markets.
